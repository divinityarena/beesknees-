Who We Are

We're GoibniuDevs — an independent development studio based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. We build tools that are fast, focused, and genuinely useful for real people.

The Bee's Knees was born out of a simple frustration: search results for "best restaurant near me" were full of paid placements, outdated reviews and chains that gamed the algorithm. We wanted something honest. Something that just told you the actual best option. So we built it.

"No noise. Just the best."

Our mission is to help people — locals, tourists, and everyone in between — instantly find the highest-quality spots near them, without wading through sponsored results, irrelevant listings, or inflated ratings. One search. Top five. Done.
